One interesting fact about yourself:
One interesting fact about me is I am a Zumba instructor!

Why did you choose UMBC-Shady Grove?
I chose UMBC-Shady Grove for multiple reasons. As a transfer student, I wanted something that was economical as well as convenient, yet provided me with top-of-the-line knowledge. The first reason I chose UMBC-Shady Grove was the location. The Universities at Shady Grove is convenient to me, making my commute to campus hassle-free.  Secondly, tuition is much more manageable and affordable especially when you are provided so many scholarship opportunities. Thirdly, attending UMBC-Shady Grove means that I maintain my jobs, all due to the convenient location, flexibility, and amenities provided.

In what internships or extracurricular activities have you been involved in at UMBC-Shady Grove?
As a student at UMBC-Shady Grove I have become involved in three extracurricular activities. As a psychology major, I found much interest in joining the Psychology Student Association, which strives to unite other psychology majors and takes part in community service activities. I then joined the Tau Sigma Honor Society which holds many fundraisers for the UMBC-Shady Grove community as well as the local community. Lastly, I am part of this wonderful Peer Advisory team (PAT) which helps the members develop leadership skills along with helping to recruit and welcome new students into the UMBC Shady-Grove community.

What are your plans following graduation?
After I graduate from UMBC, I plan to continue my education in the field of psychology striving to obtain my Master’s Degree and PhD with the goal of becoming a clinical psychologist.

What advice would you provide to a prospective student?
My advice to prospective students is to become informed and involved. The Universities at Shady Grove provides its students with many opportunities and resources to help us reach our goals as well as make college a little easier. Secondly, I would strongly advise students to get involved in anything in which they have an interest.  It will provide you a chance to learn new things, take part in many great activities as well as meet new and great people.
